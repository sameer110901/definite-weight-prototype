# definite-weight-prototype
Definite Weight™: Precision Fueling &amp; CO2 Optimization Definite Weight™ is a deep-tech aviation solution designed to eliminate the "Ghost Weight" inefficiencies inherent in commercial flight operations.By replacing outdated regulatory averages with real-time, precision-measured passenger and baggage data, we enable airlines to optimize fueling, maximize cargo revenue, and ensure compliance with the 2026 ReFuelEU and EU ETS mandates.

The Problem: The "Ghost Weight" Penalty
Under current EASA CAT.OP.MPA.150 regulations, airlines typically rely on a standard passenger weight of 84 kg. Our analysis reveals that when the true average is 78 kg, a 160-seat A320neo is forced to carry 960 kg of "Ghost Weight" per flight.

Operational Inefficiency: This weight triggers a 3.5% Cost of Weight (CoW) fuel penalty across critical EASA fuel buckets (Trip, Contingency, Alternate, and Reserve).

Financial Bleed: For a 100-aircraft fleet, this "data blindness" results in a minimum annual OpEx loss of $3.7 Million in wasted fuel alone.

Carbon Penalties: In 2026, this inefficiency generates over $470,000 in CORSIA and EU ETS carbon tax penalties, which now face full auctioning and stricter enforcement.

The Solution: Precision Fueling API
Our prototype integrates live sensor data (WIM/Lidar) with a Precision Fueling API to provide an Actual Zero Fuel Weight (AZFW) that is significantly more accurate than standard estimates.

Dynamic Load Vector Analysis: Automatically calculates real-time weight variance during boarding.

Revenue Unlock: By removing unnecessary fuel buffers, we unlock millions in constrained cargo capacity, estimated at an average of $4.00 per kg of recovered payload.

Regulatory Compliance: Our model provides the audit-ready data required for the ReFuelEU 90% fuel uplift rule, helping airlines avoid heavy non-compliance fines.

2026 Strategic Impact
With the phase-out of free emission allowances and the introduction of stricter ReFuelEU anti-tankering penalties, precision is no longer an option—it is a survival requirement. Definite Weight™ bridges the gap between legacy safety margins and the data-driven future of sustainable aviation.
